How Do Amazon KDP Printing Costs Work?

Amazon paperback print pricing is determined by page count and ink type. In the US marketplace, for standard black & white paperbacks containing 24 to 108 pages, the printing cost is a flat rate of **$2.30**. For paperbacks exceeding 108 pages, KDP applies a base charge of **$1.00** plus **$0.012 per page**. By utilizing an optimized kdp calculator, you can accurately budget these costs to ensure your list price remains competitive while maximizing net earnings [7].

Calculating Spine Width and Cover Size

A common mistake self-publishers make is designing cover files with incorrect dimensions, which leads to upload errors on the KDP Bookshelf. Our built-in cover calculator computes the exact spine width of your paperback. For standard white paper, the spine grows by **0.00225 inches** per page. Frequently Asked Questions

What is the standard KDP paperback royalty rate?

Amazon KDP pays a flat 60% royalty rate on paperbacks sold on its marketplaces. To find your royalty, calculate 60% of your list price and then subtract the printing costs.

Why does KDP cover calculation include bleed?

Bleed (0.125 inches on all four outer edges) ensures that when the printing machine cuts your physical book pages, no blank white edges are left showing on your final paperback design.
