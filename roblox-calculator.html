What is the Roblox Marketplace Fee (Tax)?

In the Roblox ecosystem, when a developer sells shirts, pants, gamepasses, or developer products, Roblox takes a standard 30% marketplace fee (commonly referred to by players as the **Roblox tax**). This leaves the developer with 70% of the revenue. Utilizing a precise roblox tax calculator ensures you always set the exact catalog pricing to reach your desired in-game profit goals.

How to Calculate Roblox Tax Manually (Formula)

Understanding the mathematics behind the robux tax calculator is simple. There are two primary formulas depending on your calculation perspective:

  • Finding your payout: If you list a gamepass for Price P, your payout will be: Payout = P * 0.70
  • Finding the required listing price: If you want to receive a specific amount Desired D, the price to list it in the store is: Listing Price = D / 0.70

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum Robux required to cash out (DevEx)?

To qualify for the Roblox Developer Exchange program, you must have a minimum of 30,000 earned Robux in your account, be a Premium member, and have a verified account in good standing.

Why does Roblox take a 30% tax?

The 30% marketplace fee is retained by Roblox to support global server hosting, maintenance, engine development, payment processing fees, and platform infrastructure overhead.
