Understanding TikTok's Economy: Coins vs. Diamonds

TikTok has revolutionized how creators monetize their content through live streams and virtual gifts. To understand how the tiktok coin calculator works, it is important to first distinguish between the primary virtual currencies inside the app:

  • TikTok Coins: The primary buyer currency. Fans buy coins with real fiat money and use them to purchase virtual gifts (like Roses, Leon the Lion, or Universe gifts) to send to creators.
  • TikTok Gift Points (Diamonds): The creator payout currency. When a creator receives a gift during a stream, it instantly converts into TikTok Gift Points (also called Diamonds). These points measure a creator's popularity and can be converted back to real money.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum amount of money I can withdraw on TikTok?

Generally, TikTok enforces a minimum payout threshold of $10 USD (which equals 2,000 Gift Points) up to a maximum limit of $1,000 USD per day, depending on your region and connected payment method (PayPal, Bank Transfer).

Can I convert my Gift Points back into Coins to send to other creators?

Yes, in many regions, TikTok allows creators to exchange their earned Diamonds/Gift Points directly back into Coins to spend on other live streams, often bypassing standard transaction fees.
